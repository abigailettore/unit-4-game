Ice Cream Collector!

Instructions

  • There are four flavors of ice cream:
    • vanilla
    • pistachio
    • strawberry
    • chocolate
  • By clicking on each flavor, you will add to the total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each ice cream is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each flavor.
